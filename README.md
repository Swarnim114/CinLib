# CinLib

A portable, lightweight, and ultra-fast library management software for your local movies and series.

---

##  What it is

**CinLib** turns your local media folders into a personalized, Netflix-style streaming catalog. Just open a folder with the app, and it instantly displays all your movies and TV series in a modern grid view.

Clicking on any title opens a detailed info page—similar to Netflix or Amazon Prime—showing descriptions, posters, and metadata, with a one-click option to play the video directly in your favorite native media player like **vlc** or **mpv**.

---

##  Key Characteristics

* **Zero Setup:** Point it at a folder and it works.
* **Netflix Experience:** Clean, modern catalog and detailed overlay screens.
* **Native Playback:** Launches videos directly into external players (**mpv**, **vlc**, etc.).
* **Ultra-Fast & Portable:** Lightweight architecture with zero bloat and instant loading.
